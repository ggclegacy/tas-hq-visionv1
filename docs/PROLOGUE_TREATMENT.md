# Prologue Creative Treatment

Status: approved on 2026-07-21 for Phase 2 implementation.

## Direction

The Prologue is a 54-second, directed executive opening created specifically for Blair Vidrine, Bailey Soileau, and The Apothecary Shoppe. It establishes exclusivity, credibility, and emotional weight without marketing language. The visual language is controlled darkness, warm architectural light, preserved brand marks, centered typography, and deliberate negative space.

The experience is complete when silent. The approved temporary narration exists as timed metadata and captions, but no voice is synthesized and no unavailable audio is required. Essential, enhanced, and cinematic tiers keep identical copy, order, and timing. Reduced motion uses static compositions and short opacity crossfades—never movement-dependent meaning.

## Asset and tier contract

- `gac-logo.png`: presenting-credit sequence only; original and public copy remain byte-identical.
- `tas-hq-logo.png`: emblem reveal and handoff only; original and public copy remain byte-identical.
- Temporary narration and ambience: manifest hooks are marked pending and are never loaded in the default experience.
- Essential: typography, preserved marks, flat architectural fields.
- Enhanced: adds depth, restrained glow, and light texture.
- Cinematic: adds controlled parallax-like depth and light passes using CSS only.

The handoff is a truthful boundary. It contains no navigation, dashboard, product demonstration, exploration shell, or implemented Act I scene.
